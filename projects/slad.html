Overview

As part of my Bachelor’s thesis, titled Street Lamp Area Detection through Classical Vision and Convolutional Neural Networks, which was in cooperation with Aptiv, I developed a component that processed pre-recorded footage to assess lighting conditions in real-time.

For each moment of the footage, the system determined whether there was sufficient lighting. This functionality allows for the automatic activation of a car’s high beams in low-light conditions and deactivation when adequate surrounding light is detected.

Skills Leveraged

  • Python
  • Computer Vision (Classical & Deep Learning)
  • Pytorch for Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
  • OpenCV for image processing
