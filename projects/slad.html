Overview

As part of my Bachelor’s thesis, titled Street Lamp Area Detection through Classical Vision and Convolutional Neural Networks, I developed a system that processes video footage to assess real-time lighting conditions.

The system determines whether there is sufficient lighting at any given moment, allowing for automatic activation of a car’s high beams in low-light conditions.

  • Computer Vision (Classical & Deep Learning)
  • PyTorch for CNNs
  • OpenCV for image processing
