Overview

This project explores reinforcement learning with Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) to train an autonomous car for obstacle-free navigation toward a goal. The agent optimizes its path through trial and error, refining its strategy over time.

Key Skills

  • Python & Reinforcement Learning
  • PyTorch for Deep Learning
  • Simulation & Training Optimization
