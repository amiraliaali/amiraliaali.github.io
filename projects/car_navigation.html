Overview

This project implements a reinforcement learning approach using Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) to train an autonomous agent (a free-moving car) to navigate a space with obstacles and reach a predefined goal spot without collisions.

The agent learns through trial and error, improving its navigation strategy over time.
